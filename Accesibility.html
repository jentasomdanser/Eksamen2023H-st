In this page we demonstrate the acceisibility and one of our most significant choices to create safe accesibility for all type of screen sizes.

We chose to merge the nav bar into a hamburger menu under 1100px width screen size. Top navigation bar choices were made based on most of our references of working websites. It makes the usability more intuitive since most users are already used to this set up. We followed the heriatic laws of UX design with this option. Making this design eliminate the need to search or learn a new usability flow. By mading the nav bar reactive to size, we enhance accesibility of our menu bar when the screen is in phone size. This means that when the page is resized with css code of media@ the content gets adjusted to new properties defined in that css. In our case it means we have a hamburger menu, that unfolds when clicking on top nav bar. This makes our accesibility adaptable to several screen sizes.
