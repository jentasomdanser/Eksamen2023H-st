Dragons Enroll in Oslo Metropolitan University's "Fire Breathing 101" Course

Oslo, October 24, 2023 - In an astonishing turn of events, dragons have been spotted soaring over Oslo Metropolitan University (OMU) campus, and they're not here to terrorize the city. Instead, they've enrolled in OMU's prestigious "Fire Breathing 101" course.

The news of these mythical creatures attending the university has taken the world by storm, with experts and enthusiasts flocking to Oslo to witness the spectacle.

Professor Elira Moonshadow, the renowned dragonologist leading the course, is thrilled by this unprecedented enrollment. She said, "Dragons are magnificent beings with an untapped potential for contributing to our world. By teaching them to control their fire-breathing abilities responsibly, we hope to foster a new era of cooperation between humans and dragons."

Local businesses have also jumped on the dragon bandwagon, offering dragon-friendly services, such as tailoring extra-large textbooks, designing customized flight suits for dragon-sized students, and hosting exclusive "Dragon Diners" for these new scholars.

Dragons aren't the only fantastical creatures making their mark at OMU. Rumor has it that a group of friendly giants is considering enrolling in the "Civil Engineering for Giants" program, while a curious centaur has been spotted exploring the university's sprawling campus.

As the world watches in amazement, Oslo Metropolitan University is proving that when it comes to education, even the most extraordinary students are welcome.

In the coming months, OMU is planning to launch a unique study abroad program, inviting students from Elven kingdoms, Dwarf strongholds, and merfolk cities to join the academic community in Oslo.

So, keep your eyes on the skies and your textbooks close; who knows what magical beings will be spotted at OMU next?
